RoverOS

About RoverOS:
RoverOS is a hobby OS intended for x64 UEFI platforms
The name, and my username, derive from my dog named Rover.
It is not intended to be named after my username.

Future plans:
* ARM64 support (I don't have an ARM device yet)
* Custom FS with EXT4 support
* Custom libc (not POSIX compliant)
* Easy GUI library
* Better debugging
